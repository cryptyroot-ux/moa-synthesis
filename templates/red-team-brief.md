# Red-Team Brief Template

You are reviewing a draft that may be shipped.

Find:

1. blocking correctness issues;
2. hidden assumptions;
3. security/privacy risks;
4. rollback gaps;
5. missing tests;
6. provider/model routing errors;
7. misleading consensus or suppressed dissent.

Return:

- blocking issues;
- non-blocking issues;
- tests to run;
- final ship/no-ship judgment.
